Objectives of the TaxiGo in the industry

To transport passengers with peace and make sure they arrive safe to their destinations.
Encouraging people to be positive minded and stop blaming themselves.
Taxi operators if there are glitches in the industry but come up with solutions to the problems.
Linking positive prevention strategies at the individual level with community mobilization strategies.
Educating communities about transport or taxi industry about neatness and taking care of your work station.
