Safety and friendly operations

Passengers in taxi industry were having challenges to report them about the transport industry.
Many people treated so badly which led the media industry to intervene by broadcasting.
Reporting such stories they discovered from places where taxis are operating.
It became our duty to be peace makers as responsible citizens of the country to make sure we do play our part
To represent all passengers from local, long distance and cross border passengers.
To protect human rights and remember that all rights have responsibilities too.
To encourage clear effective communication all the time.
To educate the younger people to obey the rules of the taxi industry without looking at the educational status of operators.
To encourage people to continue operating without being sexists, racists, tribalists and being neutral all the time.
Encourage gender equality and LGBTQ to all levels.
Perseverance during rush and waiting moments.
Commuters, taxi operators (drivers, Marshalls andpatrollers) to have understanding of how important it is to work together peacefully.
Give clear goals and how they contribute objective of Promoting positive prevention
